# Global-Tech-Layoffs-and-Hiring-Trends-Analysis-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the evolving workforce landscape within the global technology industry by examining the relationship between layoffs, hiring demand, AI adoption, and workforce health.

Using Power BI, I developed an interactive dashboard designed to support executive decision-making and uncover meaningful business insights from workforce-related data.

## Objectives

* Analyze layoff patterns across industries, companies, and market conditions.
* Identify emerging hiring trends and in-demand roles.
* Assess the impact of AI adoption on workforce dynamics.
* Evaluate workforce health using employee sentiment and job security indicators.
* Deliver actionable insights through interactive and executive-friendly dashboards.

## Dashboard Pages

1. Executive Overview
2. Layoffs Analysis
3. Hiring Trends
4. AI Impact Analysis
5. Workforce Health

## Key Insights

* The technology sector experienced a net workforce reduction of approximately **25 million jobs**, with **60 million layoffs compared to 35 million open roles**.
* AI-related workforce disruption affected approximately **12 million jobs**, while the AI sector generated **9.34 million open positions**.
* Demand for **Machine Learning Engineers (8.02M openings)** and **Data Scientists (7.75M openings)** significantly outpaced traditional technology roles.
* Recessionary periods recorded **40.85M layoffs**, compared to only **5.09M during bull markets**.
* Workforce stability varied considerably across industries, with Gaming showing the strongest resilience and Social Media facing the highest instability.
